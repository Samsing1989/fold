Charitable organization Charitable Foundation

"The power of the future of Ukraine"

In order to provide assistance to military personnel and family members of the deceased, servicemen who were maimed in the war during protection Motherland by the Public Organization "Veterans of War and participants of hostilities in the city of Chop" created a charity organization Charitable Foundation
"The power of the future of Ukraine"

Donation support

Our goal:

  • support of the military who protect our Ukraine (on the front lines) necessary equipment and machinery:
    body armor, radio stations, helmets, tactical clothing, special military first aid kits, backpacks, reconnaissance equipment, drones, cars and other;
  • assistance to the families of servicemen who died in battles;
  • providing aid to civilians affected by the war;
  • assistance to servicemen who were maimed in the war;
  • assistance to families forced to leave their homes in the districts hostilities;
  • humanitarian aid to regions of active hostilities, released from the occupation of the territory and for internally displaced persons:
    products, baby food, hygiene products, clothes, sleeping bags, medicines, etc.;
  • help in the rear, so that the work of our defenders at the front is done safer and more efficient.

The main principles of the Fund:

  • open fundraising;
  • transparent reporting on the use of funds and volunteering help

Responsibility at work:

  • every penny and every thing you have successfully transferred to us delivered to those who turned to us for help!!!

Our work and your support bring us closer to VICTORY and preserving the integrity of our Ukraine.

We invite interested citizens of Ukraine, volunteers and to support entrepreneurs (FOP), international donors and patrons Charitable organization Charitable Fund "POWER OF THE FUTURE UKRAINE"

Establishment of the fund

Charitable organization Charitable Foundation "Force of the Future of Ukraine" began its work on March 31, 2023. Head Myroslav Nikolayev was elected to the organization.

Our army defends us every day at the cost of its own life freedom, democracy and the future of our children. After all, Ukraine is a free and independent state that will remain so thanks to our strong army. We will always protect values of our country, together we will reach VICTORY!

On the occasion of the launch of the BO BF fund "Power of the future of Ukraine" Acknowledgments were presented to the Mayor of Chop and the staff of the city council, the Department of Labor and Social Affairs of population protection, to the Administration of the Center for Provision administrative services and Public organization "Organization of Ukrainian veterans of Chop Zakarpatska oblast".
